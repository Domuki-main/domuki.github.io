In this essay, I’m going to talk about how I make my notes.

What U need?

You just need a brain to think, a hand to record and something to record your knowledge and intelligence.

Choose the way to write

Compared with writing on paper, I prefer to record my notes on electric devices, such as phone, laptop or computer.

But, why?

1. Without limitation of time and space

As a college student, I need take a lot of subjects every term and we also need prepare many of notebooks for all different subjects. So, getting all of them with me to go home is not a good idea. Especially for the people have to take a long distance travel. I need to fly 4000+ KM from my college to my city. So they only allow me to take maximum 20 kilograms baggage to fly.

2. Easier to Change

For example: if you use your paper or notebook to write your note. The words you wrote are hard to change but if you use pencils. However, changing the words wrote by pen and paper will be like:

this is my world word

and also hard to add something inside the sentence:

this is my note of science

this is my friend’s notebook of computer science.

and also the order of your words. like something should be write at first line but you write it at the end…

However, if you use electric devices to write your notes, everything changing will be easier.

3. Various fonts and colours

You can write various fonts without any practices. And you can choose any colour you like without cost of colour markers.

4. Long storage time

Sometimes, I lost my notes somewhere in my college and that is hard to find back. Paper material is easier to get damages when you use it frequently.

But if you submit your notes on the Cloud Drives, it can be kept forever unless the company provide cloud to you is closed.

5. Easy to get more information

If there’re something on your notes but you don’t know, you just need to copy and search on the internet without to type it.

6. Various ways

You can record the video as the review tutorials when you have just learnt the new knowledge.

You also can write it as a blog sharing on the internet to discuss with other people.

Feeling bored when typing on keyboard? You even can use voice input program to help you write. What the thing you only need do is just speaking.

The principles

1. Faster

Don’t spend too much your time on writing note but on enjoying your life. Except your pens and keyboard, you can also use your microphone to record your note.

2. Simpler

That will save your much time when you writing or reading notes.

3. More personal

Using your own language or word is an effective way to write notes and it’s more understandable when you review it.

4. Accurate

Any simplification and summary should be based on the accuracy.

5. Make it like a speech draft (not necessary)

Teaching others is the most effective way to learn

Actually, we don’t have to many opportunities to teach others. But we still can use the way of teaching like doing a presentation to yourself.

The Steps

  1. Get a new paper/page and title it.
  2. Write the date and tags. (which helps you know when you write and what you write)
  3. Draw a conclusion.
  4. List the reasons.
  5. Give examples.
  6. Selection keywords and important points.

Review time

According to the forgotten curve, we’d better to review at:

  • 1st after 20 min
  • 2nd after a day
  • 3rd after 7 days
  • 4th after 31 days
  • 5th after 3 months

Note Codes

In order to save the time when I typing my notes, I’d like to use NOTE CODES to represent some words frequently used. Like:

  • DEF Definition
  • +AD Advantage
  • -AD Disadvantage
  • REF Reference
  • CON Conclusion
  • e.g. Exempli gratia (For example)
  • i.e. Id set (In other word)
  • etc et cetera (and so on)
