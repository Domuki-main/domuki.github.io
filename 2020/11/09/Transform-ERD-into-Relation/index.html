Transform ERD into Relation

Author: Maa ZiJyuan Date: November 9, 2020  21:04:26 Category: Software Design

For this part, I’ll get started from this question.

Figure the Supertype/subtype relationships according to the diagram below.

Answer:

  1. Every Entity need to be written as a relationship. (like every line in Answer)
  2. If there’s a many-to-many relationship between two entities, the relationship will become a entity. (like PEREQ)
  3. If there’s a one-to-many relationship between two entities, the entity in “many” side need to add a relationship of the “one” side with a dashed underline. (like lectID in STUDENT and deptID in LECTURER)
  4. Dashed circle will not be included in relationships. (like totalCr)
  5. Every primary key in Relationships need a underline.
